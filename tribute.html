Albert Einstein: A Tribute

Celebrating the life and legacy of one of the most influential scientists of all time.

Biography

Albert Einstein was born in Ulm, Germany, in 1879. Known for his contributions to physics, particularly the theory of relativity, he is widely regarded as one of the greatest scientific minds in history.

Major Achievements

  • Developed the theory of relativity
  • Won the Nobel Prize in Physics in 1921 for the photoelectric effect
  • Contributed to the development of quantum mechanics and the atomic bomb (indirectly)

Famous Quotes

"Life is like riding a bicycle. To keep your balance, you must keep moving."
"Imagination is more important than knowledge. For knowledge is limited, whereas imagination embraces the entire world."
